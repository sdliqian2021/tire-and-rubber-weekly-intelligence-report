# About

The **Tire and Rubber Weekly Intelligence Report** is a public weekly briefing for tire companies, material and equipment suppliers, researchers, and other industry professionals.

The publication follows developments connected to China's tire and rubber industries across four categories, with a focus on new technologies, especially those related to digital transformation—a topic I am very interested in:

- manufacturing;
- R&D;
- supply chains;
- marketing and sales.

## How reports are produced

Each report is created through a human-governed workflow:

- **Hardcoded rules** define the reporting period, categories, source requirements, validation checks, file naming, and approval gate.
- **AI agents** collect and assess public information and prepare the draft under those rules.
- **A human editor** reviews the draft, makes or requests changes when needed, and explicitly approves it before publication.

Every public report is supposed to pass the human-approval step, **but I cannot guarantee that—sometimes I am just lazy. :)**

Reports summarize publicly available information and link to their sources. They are provided for industry intelligence and do not constitute legal, investment, or technical advice.

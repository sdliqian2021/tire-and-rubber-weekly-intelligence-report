## Manufacturing

### Wanli and Berjaya plan a $320 million Malaysia tire hub

*Published 2026-07-09 | Wanli Tire and Berjaya Property*

**What happened:** The partners signed a joint-venture agreement for a 67.9-acre plant in Bukit Tagar, Selangor, with planned annual capacity of 5 million PCR tires and 1.2 million TBR tires and more than 1,000 jobs.

**New trend, model, material, or technology:** Wanli is moving from a single overseas site toward a multi-hub ASEAN manufacturing network built around high-performance, eco-friendly and NEV tires.

**Why it matters:** The proposed 6.2-million-tire export hub is material new capacity and shows how Chinese producers are localizing production for market access and supply-chain resilience.

**Sources:** [Wanli Tire](https://www.wanli-global.com/news/details_478_796.html) | [The Edge Malaysia](https://theedgemalaysia.com/node/809512)

## R&D and Technology

### ZC Rubber and HIT win a state award for an integrated tire technology stack

*Published 2026-07-08 | ZC Rubber, Harbin Institute of Technology and partners*

**What happened:** The High-performance Tire Key Technology and Its Green Intelligent Manufacturing project received second prize in China's State Science and Technology Progress Awards.

**New trend, model, material, or technology:** HIT disclosed a multi-information-fusion tire design method, digital analysis and optimization software, SiO2/C and CNT/C micro-nano composites, green manufacturing processes and core equipment, including aviation-tire application.

**Why it matters:** This is a rare full-stack advance spanning materials, digital design, software, equipment and production rather than treating product and factory R&D separately.

**Sources:** [Harbin Institute of Technology](https://news.hit.edu.cn/2026/0708/c11508a242822/page.htm) | [China's Ministry of Science and Technology](https://www.most.gov.cn/cxfw/kjjlcx/kjjl2025/202607/t20260708_196987.html)

### China tire forum points to bio-materials, electric curing and AI-assisted R&D

*Published 2026-07-06 | CRIA forum participants including Linglong, Aeolus, Double Coin and suppliers*

**What happened:** CRIA published the technical disclosures from its June 25 Global Tire Technology Forum covering materials, formulation, simulation, testing and production.

**New trend, model, material, or technology:** Examples included epoxidized natural rubber, itaconic-acid bio-based rubber, renewable fillers, low-hysteresis carbon black, AI material simulation, real-time laser wear scanning, electric-heating vulcanization and a tire using 90% sustainable materials.

**Why it matters:** The disclosures show sustainability moving from isolated substitutions toward coordinated change across compounds, curing, testing and digital formulation.

**Sources:** [China Rubber Industry Association](https://www.cria.org.cn/a/2074024351431233538)

### Triangle Tire publishes RFID-barcode binding and rejection patent application

*Published 2026-07-07 | Triangle Tire*

**What happened:** Patent application CN122347166A was published for automatically linking an RFID tag embedded in a green tire with its production barcode after curing.

**New trend, model, material, or technology:** Panoramic barcode scanning and RFID reading send both identities to MES; rule validation creates a one-to-one record and failed matches trigger automatic line rejection.

**Why it matters:** The method closes a traceability gap between the physical tire, embedded digital identity and manufacturing record, supporting automated quality control and future product passports.

**Sources:** [Spanish Patent and Trademark Office technology alert](https://www.oepm.es/en/informacion-tecnologica/vigilancia-tecnologica/alertas-tecnologicas/alerta?id=851&page=31) | [Google Patents](https://patents.google.com/patent/CN122347166A/zh)

### Chinese PA66 reinforcement reaches industrial-scale aviation-tire application

*Published 2026-07-10 | Shuguang Rubber Institute, Shenma Industrial, Zhejiang Sci-Tech University and partners*

**What happened:** A project on continuous-polymerization melt direct-spinning of high-performance PA66 industrial filament and its use in premium tires received a national science and technology progress second prize.

**New trend, model, material, or technology:** The team linked industrial-scale high-viscosity PA66 direct spinning to cord fabric adapted for aircraft tires, reporting higher load and impact performance with lower tire weight.

**Why it matters:** Localized, safety-critical aviation-tire reinforcement strengthens China's materials supply chain and demonstrates transfer from polymer process engineering into a validated tire application.

**Sources:** [Sinochem Haohua / Shuguang Rubber Institute](https://haohua.sinochem.com/haohua/xwzx/gsxw/2026/7/I1525064924701655040.html) | [China's Ministry of Science and Technology](https://www.most.gov.cn/cxfw/kjjlcx/kjjl2025/202607/t20260708_196987.html)

## Supply Chain

### Bekaert and CITIC Special Steel launch closed-loop tire-steel project

*Published 2026-07-08 | Bekaert and CITIC Pacific Special Steel*

**What happened:** The companies signed a technical-cooperation agreement to test whether steel recovered from end-of-life tires can be remade into wire rod and then new tire-reinforcement products.

**New trend, model, material, or technology:** The early proof of concept combines joint studies, industrial trials and material evaluation for high shares of closed-loop steel without sacrificing required quality or performance.

**Why it matters:** Successful tire-to-tire steel recovery would move recycling beyond downcycling, reduce virgin-steel demand and give tire makers a traceable circular reinforcement input.

**Sources:** [Bekaert](https://www.bekaert.com/en/about-us/news-room/news/2026/bekaert-and-citic-special-steel-sign-strategic-cooperation-agree) | [European Rubber Journal](https://www.european-rubber-journal.com/article/2099421/chinese-steel-group-bekaert-launch-close-loop-recycled-tire-steel-project)

### Trade barriers and freight costs weaken Chinese tire export orders

*Published 2026-07-07 | Chinese PCR and TBR producers*

**What happened:** Longzhong reported month-on-month export-order declines at 67% of its semi-steel sample enterprises and 64% of its all-steel sample, alongside high freight costs and softer domestic demand.

**New trend, model, material, or technology:** Export friction and logistics inflation are translating into maintenance, production restraint and efforts to redirect sales toward markets such as Africa.

**Why it matters:** The signal matters upstream: weaker run rates can quickly reduce demand for rubber, carbon black, chemicals, reinforcement and equipment while changing shipment and inventory plans.

**Sources:** [Longzhong Information](https://www.oilchem.net/26-0707-11-afbbd8def131d482.html)

## Marketing and Sales

### EU duties reset the economics of Chinese PCR and light-truck tire exports

*Published 2026-07-09 | Chinese tire exporters and the European Commission*

**What happened:** The EU imposed definitive anti-dumping duties of 4.3% to 45.3% on China-origin passenger-car and light-lorry tires; the measures took effect on July 8 and a parallel anti-subsidy case remains open.

**New trend, model, material, or technology:** Trade barriers are accelerating localized production, market diversification, higher-value products and lower dependence on price-led direct exports from China.

**Why it matters:** China supplied almost 93 million covered tires worth more than EUR 2.5 billion in 2024, so the duties have immediate consequences for pricing, margins, channels and volume.

**Sources:** [European Commission](https://policy.trade.ec.europa.eu/news/commission-imposes-anti-dumping-duties-passenger-car-and-light-lorry-tyres-china-2026-07-09_en) | [EUR-Lex](https://eur-lex.europa.eu/legal-content/EN/TXT?uri=CELEX%3A32026R1540)

### ZC Rubber combines premium OE wins with data-enabled deep distribution

*Published 2026-07-07 | ZC Rubber*

**What happened:** ZC said first-half domestic PCR replacement sales exceeded 10.8 million units, up more than 15%, and cited exclusive OE nominations for the Maextro S800 and AITO M6 plus more than six planned second-half premium-model launches.

**New trend, model, material, or technology:** Its channel plan combines regional service distributors, fast offline fulfillment, BI models and chips across core channel products while it moves into higher-value Chinese EV OE programs.

**Why it matters:** The model links premiumization, digital product identity and local inventory execution; it could strengthen both dealer control and consumer service speed.

**Sources:** [ZC Rubber official WeChat preserved by 10jqka](https://stock.10jqka.com.cn/20260707/c678007592.shtml) | [ZC Rubber original WeChat post](https://mp.weixin.qq.com/s?__biz=MzIwMTYyNTU5NA%3D%3D&chksm=8f23d3058b67ea5156ecf81fd379c3e02c556a9dc639b748863064887ccf4951128819b7a680&idx=1&mid=2650469663&sn=a4d332c103c8477a6a2dd1deb0085440)

### Maxxis uses independent worn-tire testing to support premium positioning

*Published 2026-07-07 | Maxxis / Cheng Shin Rubber (China)*

**What happened:** In its July 7 China-market disclosure, Maxxis said the tested 235/35 R19 91Y XL specification of the Victra Sport 6 had become the first Asian summer passenger tire awarded the TUV SUD Premium Tire Mark after independent testing against leading products.

**New trend, model, material, or technology:** The program evaluates dry and wet performance, wet grip when worn, rolling resistance, high-speed durability and exterior noise, aligning premium claims with lifecycle performance rather than new-tire performance alone.

**Why it matters:** Third-party lifecycle validation can improve export credibility, dealer confidence and pricing power as worn-tire safety becomes more important to regulation and buyers.

**Sources:** [Maxxis China](https://www.maxxis.com.cn/news_detail.php?Key=148&cID=3) | [TUV SUD](https://www.tuvsud.com/zh-tw/resource/gcn-en-pressrelease/2026/jul/maxxis-vs6-tire)
